What is ceramic coating in car detailing?

Ceramic coating is a liquid polymer applied to the exterior of a vehicle that chemically bonds with the factory paint, providing a protective layer that enhances durability, shine, and resistance to environmental contaminants.

What are the main benefits of applying ceramic coating to a car?

The main benefits include enhanced gloss and shine, increased protection against UV rays, chemical stains, oxidation, ease of cleaning due to hydrophobic properties, and resistance to minor scratches.

Does ceramic coating completely eliminate the need for waxing a car?

Yes, once a vehicle is coated with ceramic coating, traditional waxing becomes unnecessary since the coating provides superior protection and gloss. However, regular maintenance is still required.

How long does ceramic coating last on a vehicle?

The longevity of ceramic coating can vary based on the product used and maintenance practices but typically lasts from 2 to 5 years.

Can ceramic coating be applied at home or should it be done professionally?

While DIY kits are available for home application, professional installation is recommended for optimal results as it requires proper surface preparation and expertise.

What does paint correction service include?

Paint correction service removes hazing, minor scratches, swirl marks, and other blemishes on the vehicle's clear coat by polishing the surface with specialized machines and compounds.

What is included in the complete car detail package?

Complete car detail includes full exterior hand wash, 6-month paint protection, interior cleaning and sanitizing including steam cleaning carpets and upholstery, staining extraction, and showroom-quality finish.
